How to Use the Bingo Card Generator
üìù Adding Content
  • Click any square to type text directly
  • Press 'Enter' to add a line break
  • Drag and drop images onto squares
  • Paste images or text using Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac)
  • Text automatically resizes to fit the square
üé® Customizing Your Card
  • Edit the title at the top of the controls
  • Use +/- buttons to adjust rows and columns (1-10)
  • Click "Generate" to create a new grid
  • Toggle between light and dark card themes
üíæ Saving Your Work
  • Click "Save as PNG" to download your card as an image
  • The file will be named based on your card title
  • Use "Clear All" to remove all content from squares
üí° Tips

Text automatically shrinks when it reaches the sides of a square. If you add a space that would exceed the line width, the next word moves to a new line at the current font size.
