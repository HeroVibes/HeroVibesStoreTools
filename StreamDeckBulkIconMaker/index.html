Quick Guide

Rotate

Use ↺ / ↻ to rotate the currently active (most recently selected) icon in 45° steps. Rotation is stored per-icon — each icon in your selection remembers its own angle independently. The badge on the grid tile and the preview both reflect the active icon's rotation.

Duplicate

Select one or more icons then click Duplicate. Copies are added to the top of the grid. Duplicating is separate from rotating — use rotate on a duplicate just like any other icon.

Resolution

Click the Resolution card to cycle through sizes: 72px, 96px, 144px (default), 288px, 500px.

Multi-Size & ZIP

Generates all 5 sizes and packages them into a ZIP, organised into folders by resolution.

Effects

Edge On/Off: Toggle corner bracket decorations.
Edge Glow: Adds glow to brackets (requires Edge On).
Logo Glow: Adds glow behind the icon.

Content Tabs

Icons: Select or upload logos.
Textures: Add overlay patterns.
Gradients: Apply gradient backgrounds (overrides solid background colour).
