Format Mismatch!
You are uploading multiple formats (e.g. Photos and Text Files). Please clear the list and convert one "Type" at a time. Multiple files of the same type are allowed.

Troubleshooting & Tips

  • Pop-ups Blocked: When converting multiple files, your browser may ask for permission to "download multiple files". Please click Allow.
  • Text Formatting: Converting between formats like HTML to TXT will intentionally strip visual styles to preserve pure text readability.
